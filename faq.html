Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of products does your store offer?

A: We specialize in innovative lawn care solutions, including automatic lawnmowers, automatic rakes, and combination tools like rake-mower hybrids. From compact models to high-tech, eco-friendly devices, we have options to fit any yard and lifestyle.



Q: What’s the difference between the regular automatic lawnmower and the Lite version?

A: The Lite version is a smaller, faster model designed for compact lawns and quick trimming. It’s perfect for those who want efficient performance in a sleek, lightweight package.



Q: Do you have options for larger yards?

A: Absolutely! Our large-capacity automatic lawnmower is designed specifically for bigger spaces, featuring an expanded grass holding capacity to reduce the need for frequent emptying.



Q: What makes the Super Automatic Lawnmower special?

A: The Super Automatic Lawnmower is packed with high-tech features like AI navigation, app controls, obstacle detection, and weather-adaptive scheduling. It’s designed for those who want a cutting-edge, hands-off lawn care experience.



Q: Do you sell combination tools?

A: Yes! We offer a Rake-Mower Combo that handles both lawn mowing and leaf cleanup in a single pass. We also have a wind-powered version that uses natural energy to blow leaves away while mowing.



Q: What is the automatic rake, and how does it work?

A: The automatic rake gathers leaves and yard debris effortlessly, making cleanup quick and easy. For added fun, we also offer a version with a built-in radio to keep you entertained while you work!



Q: Are your products environmentally friendly?

A: Many of our products are designed with eco-friendly features, such as wind-powered functionality or efficient energy usage, to help reduce their environmental impact while delivering top-notch performance.



Q: Can I control these devices remotely?

A: Certain models, like the Super Automatic Lawnmower, come with app-based controls, allowing you to manage and customize your lawn care routine from anywhere.



Q: Are your products suitable for uneven or obstacle-filled yards?

A: Yes, our high-tech models, like the Super Automatic Lawnmower, include advanced obstacle detection and terrain adjustment features, making them ideal for uneven yards or areas with obstacles.



Q: Do you offer warranties or customer support?

A: Absolutely! All our products come with a warranty, and we’re dedicated to providing excellent customer support to ensure you’re satisfied with your purchase.
